How to Donate

Step-by-step guide to submitting donations, accepted item types, and coordination with food bank partners.

Overview

Registered users can submit donations through two channels: directly to a listed food bank organization, or through the Local Government Unit (LGU) of Guagua, Pampanga. All donations are documented and reflected in the donor's account once verified by the receiving party.

Donation Process

  1. Log in to your FoodBank App account.
  2. Choose your donation channel:
    • Via the LGU of Guagua, Pampanga
    • Directly to a registered food bank organization
  3. If donating through the LGU, coordinate with the Mayor's Office or the Local Social Welfare and Development Office (LSWDO).
  4. Complete and submit the donation report form with accurate item details.
  5. Await confirmation from the assigned office or receiving organization.
  6. Once verified, the donation will appear in the Donations section of your account.

Communicating with Organizations

Use the built-in messaging feature to coordinate directly with food bank staff — including scheduling, inquiries, and delivery logistics.

Accepted Items

The platform accepts the following donation categories:

  • Food Items — Canned goods, rice, noodles, and other shelf-stable products.
  • Clothing — Clean, wearable clothes, jackets, and blankets.
  • Cash — Monetary donations subject to LGU or organizational guidelines.
  • Medicine — Factory-sealed, unexpired medical supplies.
  • Perishable Goods — Fresh produce, cooked meals, or refrigerated items — accepted with prior coordination and approval.
  • Other — Additional items accepted at the discretion of partner organizations.

Donation Guidelines

  • All food must be unexpired, properly sealed, and safe for consumption.
  • Medicines must be factory-sealed and within their expiration date.
  • Perishable goods require prior approval and must be properly packaged for immediate distribution.
  • Clothing must be clean and in wearable condition.
  • For large or unusual donations, use the in-app messaging feature to coordinate in advance.
