Terms & Agreement

Service terms, user responsibilities, platform rules, and account conditions.

Overview

The FoodBank App is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between food donors and those in need. Our mission is to reduce food waste and ensure that surplus high-quality food reaches local communities in Guagua, Pampanga efficiently.

The Problem We Solve:

Currently, no unified digital platform exists for Guagua's food banks. This app addresses that gap by providing real-time shortage alerts, direct messaging between donors and food bank staff, and an interactive location mapping system for all participating food banks. It also directly coordinates with the Local Social Welfare and Development Office (LSWDO), which manages storage and distribution of perishable donations and relief goods.

Accounts

These terms govern everyone who accesses or uses the FoodBank App, including:

  • Community Donors
  • Donating Organizations
  • LGU Officials (such as LSWDO Staff)
  • Food Bank Personnel

Donors Accounts

Open to community members and organizations in Guagua, Pampanga (or can be outside the pampanga except in organizations). Donors can register both email and mobile number, and by default the two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled, and immediately access:

  • Donation tracking dashboard
  • Real-time shortage alerts
  • Direct messaging system
  • Donation history and profile editing

Administrator Accounts

Assigned only to Guagua LGU officials, food bank personnel, and designated LSWDO staff. Admins have elevated access including:

  • Verifying donations and donor identities
  • Updating real-time shortage lists
  • Managing food bank locations on the interactive locator
  • Overseeing all donor communications
  • Dashboard showing incoming donations and pending messages

Account Conditions

  • Accuracy: You must provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration.
  • Security: You are responsible for safeguarding your login credentials. All accounts require verification via One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your registered mobile number.
  • Eligibility: Users must be at least 18 years of age, or have parental consent, to facilitate food donations or pickups.
  • Single Account: Each individual or organization may maintain only one active account per account type.

User Responsibilities

By accessing or using our platform, you agree to abide by these Terms & Agreement. These terms are designed to maintain a safe, respectful, and transparent environment for all participants, including donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries.

To ensure the integrity of the platform and the safety of the food distributed, all users must adhere to the following rules based on their account type.

End-Users

  1. Accurate Information: Provide accurate contact information and donation details when using the app.
  2. Food Safety: Only donate food items that are non-perishable, unexpired, and properly sealed — unless otherwise coordinated with the LSWDO for perishable goods.
  3. Responsiveness: Respond to messages from food bank administrators within a reasonable time to confirm drop-offs or pickups.
  4. Cancellations: Update donation cancellations or rescheduling at least 2 hours before the agreed time.
  5. Account Security: Keep your account secure by enabling 2FA and never sharing login credentials.
  6. Incident Reporting: Report any spoilage, safety concerns, or app issues to administrators immediately.

Administrators

  1. Location Accuracy: Keep food bank locations, operating hours, and contact details accurate on the interactive locator.
  2. Shortage Updates: Update real-time shortage notifications at least once daily or whenever stock changes significantly.
  3. Communication: Acknowledge donor messages within 24 hours and coordinate drop-offs or inquiries promptly.
  4. Verification: Verify donor identities through 2FA and refuse donations that do not meet safety standards.
  5. Perishable Coordination: Coordinate with the LSWDO on perishable goods — ensure proper refrigeration and maintain distribution logs.
  6. Privacy: Maintain donor privacy by never sharing personal data (names, addresses, contact numbers) publicly.
  7. Reporting: Submit monthly donation summaries and shortage trend reports to the LGU for resource planning.

Reporting Violations

If you observe any misuse of the platform, unsafe food handling, privacy breaches, or violations of these terms, report them immediately using the in-app Report button or by contacting the LSWDO hotline directly. All reports are treated confidentially. Administrators will investigate and may suspend or permanently terminate accounts found to be in violation.

Liability Disclaimer

The FoodBank App and the Guagua LGU serve as a coordination platform only. While we strive to ensure food safety and accurate information, we cannot be held liable for the quality, condition, or fitness of donated items beyond what is verifiable at the point of collection. The LSWDO's storage and distribution guidelines serve as the binding safety standard for all perishable goods. Users who violate platform rules assume full personal responsibility for any resulting consequences.
