Privacy & Security

How we collect, protect, and handle the personal data of donors and administrators across the FoodBank App platform.

Overview

The FoodBank App is a community platform managed by the Local Government Unit (LGU) of Guagua, Pampanga. We are committed to maintaining a safe and transparent digital environment — one that connects donors with local food banks while protecting every user's personal information through industry-standard security practices.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to deliver core platform features:

  • Location Data — Used to help users find and navigate to participating food banks within Guagua.
  • Donation Records — A history of contributions tied to each donor's profile for personal tracking and impact reporting.
  • Messaging Logs — Communications between donors and staff stored to facilitate drop-off coordination and support.

Security Measures

All accounts and data are protected through the following controls:

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) — Mandatory for all users. OTP verification is required at login to confirm identity and protect account access.
  • Role-Based Access Control — Platform access is restricted to authorized administrators (Guagua LGU officials and food bank personnel) and verified registered users. No cross-role data exposure.
  • Data Integrity — Real-time shortage and donation data is validated at point of entry to ensure accuracy and prevent unauthorized modifications.
